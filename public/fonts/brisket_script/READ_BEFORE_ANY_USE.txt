Brisket Script is a friendly, flowing script with a warm, handcrafted feel.

The lettering is smooth and relaxed, with rounded strokes and a steady rhythm that makes it easy to read while still feeling personal. It carries a casual confidence, like a painted shop sign or a handwritten note pinned in a window. The shapes lean slightly forward, giving the text a sense of movement without becoming rushed or messy.

Brisket Script was drawn to feel natural and honest. It doesn’t try to impress with sharp contrast or exaggerated swashes, but instead focuses on balance and charm. The result is a script that feels familiar, approachable, and quietly expressive.

The font is built with advanced OpenType functionality and guaranteed top-notch quality, containing stylistic and contextual alternates, ligatures and more automatic and manual features; all to give you full control and customizability. 
It has extensive lingual support, covering all Latin-based languages, from North Europa to South Africa, from America to South-East Asia. It contains all characters and symbols you'll ever need, including all punctuation and numbers. 

Designed by Måns Grebäck, Brisket Script reflects an interest in everyday lettering and the small details that make text feel alive. It is shaped with care rather than force, letting the letters speak in a calm, human voice.
